History of Coffee

Coffee's journey begins in the ancient forests of Ethiopia, where legend credits its discovery to a goat herder named Kaldi. Around 850 AD, Kaldi noticed his goats becoming particularly energetic after eating red berries from a certain tree. Intrigued, he shared his findings with a local monastery, where monks brewed the first coffee, using it to stay awake during prayers.
